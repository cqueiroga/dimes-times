ABOUT ME!

Welcome to the portfolio of John Dimes!

Your rather clever digits have either purposely, or accidentally, stumbled upon the site for Author/Performer/Humorist/Graphic Designer John Thomas Dimes. As you have no doubt surmised from his picture, he is a rather photogenic individual whilst posed at a certain angle.

AND whilst being perfectly still.

When he's in MOTION?

Well. . .the less said. . . !

His written works have appeared in various and sundry anthologies. And he's the author of several books which include:   The White Corpse Hustle: A Guide For The Flegling Vampire, The Rites of Pretending Tribe, Coincidissidence, and Intracations.

Mr. Dimes's illustrated works have appeared in various comic books for such publications as Bash Magazine, Two Fold Comics, Candy Or Medicine, Omega Comics Presents, and Gurlimann's Bizarre Bazaar
